Welcome to the site all about Conor!

Hello and welcome to my website. My name is Conor Heeney and I created this site to look back on the hobbies that have shaped who I am and the experiences I have gained from them. Enjoy!

A little Introduction

My name is Conor Heeney and I was born on the 24th August in the year 1999. I am 19 years old and was born in the quaint little town of Wexford, Co. Wexford in the South East of Ireland. I am currently a first year student in University College Cork, studying computer science. I previously attended Kennedy Park Primary School and St. Peters College Secondary School in Wexford. I studied 8 subjects: English, Irish, Maths, Physics, Business, Applied Maths, French and Music.

The Leaving Cert

As I am in first year of University, I obviously completed the Leaving Cert (Just about). It was a stressful year full of tears, anxiety and sleepless nights, but I got through it and reached my dream of studying computer science in university. I recieved 477 points in my Leaving, which was well above what I needed for my course. Here's a summary of my results:

My Hobbies

I'm not a very sporty person. The only 2 sports I do are cycling and rowing. My two favourite hobbies, however, are music and drama. I started off at the young age of 6, when I began playing the violin in the County Wexford School of Music. It was very clear from early on that I would be more involved with the arts rather than sports. When I turned 7, I started in my local drama group: The Corish Wallace Performing Arts School. I didn't exactly fit in back in primary school, but these two hobbies let me be myself. Since then, I have travelled to Italy to play music, acted in front of TDs and senators in the Dail, and I am now a part of one of the biggest amature orchestras in the country; Wexford Sinfonia.

My Family

My mother's name is Mairead, who was born in Wexford and my father's name is Jimmy, who was born and raised in Co. Westmeath, just outside Mullingar. I have 3 sisters - Fiona, Niamh and Aisling. I have 4 nephews and a niece - Fiona's children, Adam (9), Dylan (4) and Cillian (1) and Niamh's children, Caoimhe (4), Liam (2). I'm the youngest of my siblings, and I'm the only boy, so I'm obviously the best.
